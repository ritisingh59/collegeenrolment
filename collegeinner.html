College Overview

JSS Academy of Technical Education, Noida (JSSATEN) is a premier technical institution established in 1998 by JSS Mahavidyapeetha. Affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Uttar Pradesh, JSSATEN is known for its excellence in technical education, research, and innovation. Located in Noida, Sector-62, the institute offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, management, and applied sciences. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and strong industry collaborations, JSSATEN ensures quality education, skill development, and excellent placement opportunities. The campus also provides modern hostels for both boys and girls, fostering a vibrant and disciplined learning environment.
